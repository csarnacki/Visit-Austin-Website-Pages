About the City of Austin

Austin is located in Central Texas on the edge of the Texas Hill Country. It is the county seat of Travis County and
is located entirely within the county. To the north of Austin are the towns of Round Rock and Georgetown which, together, help to make up
the Austin - Round Rock metro area which is the fourth largest in the state of Texas. The city has recently become
a haven for start-ups and tech companies to grow and flourish and is home to numerous companies such
as Indeed, Dell, Whole Foods, Oracle and EZCorp. The city prides itself on it's famous "Keep Austin Weird" slogan
which is meant to indicate that the city shares unique values outside of what typical Texas values would be.
Because of this, it's great culture and the mild weather it receives year-round, Austin is a city that has
become one of the fastest growing cities in the United States.
