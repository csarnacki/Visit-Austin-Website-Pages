High-End

The following hotels are classified as being on the higher end of hotels in Austin. There are no 5 star hotels in the
city, however, there are numerous 4 star hotels located mainly in downtown.

  • Austin Proper Hotel
  • Four Seasons Hotel Austin
  • Fairmont Austin
  • W Austin
  • The Driskill

Mid-Tier

The following hotels are considered average to above average. Although usually not as expensive as some of the options
listed above, a few of them can be on the more pricey side for the average consumer.

  • Hotel Saint Cecilia
  • Hyatt Regency Austin
  • Embassy Suites Austin Downtown South Congress
  • Hotel Eleven Austin
  • Canopy by Hilton Austin Downtown
  • DoubleTree Suites by Hilton Hotel Austin

Lower-Tier

The following hotels are easily affordable and offer a perfect place for a traveler to stay at if they are looking
to keep things on a tight budget. Although not as luxurious as some of the other options, these hotels are more typical
of your average chain hotel.

  • TownePlace Suites by Marriott Austin South
  • Candlewood Suites Austin Airport
  • Hotel San Jose
  • Extended Stay America-Austin-Downtown-Town Lake
  • Hyatt House Austin/Downtown
  • La Quinta Inn by Wyndham Austin Capitol/Downtown
